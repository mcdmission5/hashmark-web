Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Hashmark does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts and no login — you can browse everything without identifying yourself in any way.

✓What the app shows

Hashmark displays publicly available college-football data and our own computed ratings, fetched from our database over a secure, read-only connection. It does not read anything from your device or your accounts.

✓No advertising, no tracking

We do not use advertising or third-party tracking. There are no analytics SDKs, ad networks, or trackers embedded in the app.

✓On-device caching only

The app may cache content on your device for offline use and performance. This never leaves your device and is used solely to make Hashmark load fast and work offline.

✓Independent & gambling-free

Hashmark is gambling-free and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any school, conference, the NCAA, or the AP.
