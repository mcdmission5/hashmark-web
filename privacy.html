Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

You can use all of Hashmark without an account. Every stat, leaderboard, standings page, split, matchup and the simulator is free and requires no sign-in — browse anonymously and we collect no personal information about you. Hashmark now also offers an optional account so you can personalize your experience (for example, saving a favorite team). This policy explains what we collect only if you choose to sign in.

✓What we collect (only with an account)

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Your email address and sign-in identity — from email/password or magic-link sign-in, or from Google sign-in (we receive your email and basic Google profile via OAuth; we never receive or store your Google password).
  • A display name (defaults from your sign-in; you may change it).
  • Your favorite team and saved preferences.
  • In the future, your pick'em selections when that feature launches.

If you don't sign in, we collect none of the above. We never read your contacts, location, photos, or other device data.

✓Why we collect it

Solely to provide your account and personalized features — to keep you signed in, show your favorite team on your home page, and (later) save your picks. That's it.

✓Never sold, no ads, no tracking

We do not sell or rent your data, and we never use it for advertising. There are no ad networks, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers embedded in Hashmark.

✓Where your account data is stored (Supabase)

Accounts, authentication, and your saved data are handled by Supabase, our database and authentication provider acting as our data processor, over secure encrypted connections. Passwords are managed by Supabase Auth and are never visible to us. Your account data is protected by row-level security so that only you can access your own account record.

✓Delete your account and data

You can delete your account and all associated data (profile, favorite team, preferences, and any saved picks) at any time by emailing support@hash-mark.com from your account email — we'll remove it promptly. (In-app self-service deletion is coming.) You can also simply sign out at any time to stop using your account.

✓On-device caching only

The app caches content (and, if you're signed in, your session) on your device for performance and offline use. This stays on your device and is used solely to make Hashmark load fast and keep you signed in between visits.

✓Independent & gambling-free

Hashmark shows team & player names and statistics only — no team logos, no player photos. It is gambling-free (no betting lines, spreads, or wagering advice) and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any school, conference, the NCAA, or the AP.
